## 2026-07-17 editorial pass (20 findings; 19 approved, 1 denied)

### R3 — Ability boosts: 4th & 8th, or 5th & 10th? — DENIED (resolved the other way)

**The conflict.** Player's Book Ch. IX granted one ability point at 4th and 8th
level; Ch. XIV instead granted "four ability boosts at 5th and 10th level, each
raising a different ability a step" — two different advancement schemes.

**Proposed (denied).** Standardize on Ch. IX's timing: "One ability score raised
by a point at 4th and at 8th level."

**Cole's decision.** Denied, with note: *"I would like one ability score raised
by a point at 5th and 10th level."*

**What was applied instead (v2.13).** The single-point scheme was kept but moved
to Ch. XIV's timing — both chapters now read one ability score raised by a point
at **5th and 10th** level (Ch. IX intro and Ch. XIV "What a Level Brings" both
updated). Nothing here is pending; this entry records that the original proposal
was rejected in favor of the 5th/10th resolution, in case the timing is ever
revisited (e.g. if higher-level play support changes the advancement curve).
